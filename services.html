Our Services

We provide end-to-end manufacturing and engineering solutions designed to support your business at every stage of the project lifecycle � from concept to delivery.

Manufacturing Services

We offer precision-driven manufacturing solutions using advanced technologies and skilled expertise:

  • CNC turning and milling
  • Drilling, boring, and threading
  • Heat treatment coordination
  • Surface finishing as per client requirements

Our focus is on delivering high-quality components with accuracy and consistency.

Quality & Inspection

Quality is at the core of everything we do. We ensure strict compliance with industry standards:

  • Material Test Certificates (MTC / MTR)
  • Dimensional inspection
  • Hardness testing
  • Third-party inspection coordination (TPI)
  • Compliance with ASTM, ASME, API, and NACE standards

We guarantee reliable and certified products that meet global benchmarks.

Engineering & Technical Support

Our engineering team provides complete technical assistance to optimize your product design and manufacturing process:

  • Design assistance and manufacturability review
  • Material selection guidance
  • Cost optimization solutions
  • Documentation and technical support

We help you achieve efficient, cost-effective, and high-performance solutions.

Supply & After-Sales Support

We ensure smooth delivery and continued support even after project completion:

  • On-time delivery
  • Export packaging solutions
  • Technical clarification and customer support

Our commitment is to provide seamless service and long-term client satisfaction.
